About Me

I'm a 33-year-old entrepreneur based in Rio de Janeiro, BR. I'm CEO and co-founder of Deskovery, an web and mobile platform to book hot desks and rooms in coworking and shared office spaces. Currently, I'm also working as a part-time WordPress developer for Estúdio Touch.

I'm engaged with Lisane Andrade, I love my dogs Bingo and Thor, I enjoy watching series and movies at Netflix, cooking recipes I found in the internet and addicted to japanese food. I also love travelling and visiting new places. I play soccer once a week and walk early in the morning in the park when I can get out of bed.

Work Experience

Freelance Senior WordPress Developer - Estúdio Touch (May/2017 - Present)

Working on a project for a startup built on top of WordPress Multisites. I'm developing custom themes, plugins and a dashboard based on Zircos Material Design.

CEO & Co-Founder - Deskovery (2014 - Present)

Deskovery is an web and mobile platform to search and book office spaces. In 2014, we've launched our WordPress powered MVP.

In 2015, we rebuilt our platform with Laravel. We were accelerated at Startup NAVE, a 4-month equity-free program brought by Universidade Estácio de Sá. We were in the finals to pitch our startup in the Demo Day.

In 2016, we were selected by Benfeitoria and Youse to run a crowdfunding campaign. We raised about R$ 33.000,00 to develop our Android and iOS app (coming soon).

CEO & Co-founder - Blue Factory (2008 - Present)

Since 2008, we've been working on web development projects for several companies including Fundação Getúlio Vargas, Fiocruz and Prefeitura do Rio de Janeiro and much more.

Founder & Beekeeper - BeesOffice (2010 - 2013)

BeesOffice was the first coworking space in Rio de Janeiro. As pioneers, we spreaded the coworking culture to thousands of people that was part of our community during its 3 years of existence.

In August 9th, 2010, we've launched Coworking Day, in honor of Brad Neuberg, creator of the first coworking space in the world, 5 years before. This day was adopted by lots of coworking spaces from around the world and is celebrated every year.

BeesOffice was also a big supporter and sponsor of lots of tech and startup events, including Geeks On Beer, WordPress Meetup, and the first batch of Startup Farm in Rio de Janeiro, currently one the most successful startup accelerators in Brazil and Latin America.
